Common Issues

Here are answers to common questions:

  • Subscription Problems: If you’re having issues with your $11.99/month or $59.99/year subscription, ensure it’s managed via your app store account (Apple App Store or Google Play Store). Contact us at will@5dwaves.com for refunds, cancellations, or troubleshooting.
  • App Crashes: Update to the latest version of 5D Waves and restart your device. If the issue persists, email us at will@5dwaves.com with details.
  • Feature Access: Premium features (e.g., curated playlists, breathing visual) require an active subscription. Check your subscription status in the app or app store, or contact us for assistance.
  • Sound or Visual Issues: If playlists or the breathing visual aren’t working, ensure your device has a stable internet connection and the latest app version. Reach out for further support if needed.
  • Deleting Your Account: If you wish to delete your account, you can do it directly through the 'Delete Account' option on the profile page in app. Reach out for further support at will@5dwaves.com with details.
